About Me

Where I'm coming from

I'm a Business Management student at Brigham Young University with a competitive streak that started on the cross-country course and never left. I ran all four years in high school — earning all-conference and all-state honors — and that same discipline now shows up in how I approach work and school.

Most of my experience is in sales. I've spent four summers in door-to-door sales, worked as a pest control technician, and now support a corporate sales team. Along the way I helped build a sales organization with a $1M+ downline. I like the parts of business where you have to earn someone's trust face-to-face.

Outside of work and class, I'm a basketball fan through and through — which is exactly why my personal project pages are all about the game.
